About us

Online Bookstore is fictional online store that specialises in selling books from a limited number of authors to readers who are passionate about the work of these authors. The idea is to build a strong bond between authors and their readers.

This site is personal project to practice various features of web development and Django framework. The author's whose names and books are on this site are purely for learning purposes. I have read these books and enjoyed them and only uploaded their covers to check the functionality of the code that I have written. There is no commercial cause behind this site.
